Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

1. Usage Policy

  • This WiFi service is free for up to 2 hours of continuous use.
  • After your 2-hour session expires, the service will be unavailable for your device for 5 hours.
  • This time limitation ensures fair usage and prevents network congestion.

2. Privacy & Data

  • User account data is stored securely and not shared with third parties.
  • We support privacy-focused email addresses and alternative account identifiers.
  • Connection logs are maintained for technical and security purposes only.

3. Account Usage

  • Your account is linked to your email address. Only one active device per account is allowed.
  • Sharing account credentials with others is not recommended and may result in account suspension.
  • Multiple simultaneous logins from different locations will trigger our automatic security system.
  • Accounts detected with unusual access patterns may be temporarily or permanently suspended.
